About Us

JADA House International is a faith-based social ministry. It's a place where our community can come together and release daily social issues and at the same time elevate and worship God.There are no judgements or judging inside of our fellowship. Every week we have an agenda with a scripture referencing God. God's word is our foundation in these fellowship meetings where we've experienced healing, faith, love and most of all unity. We have inspirational speakers come through providing needed inspiration and food is provided at each meeting. JADA House International is organized by a group of ten women including the Executive Director Terri Shields.
